About Orizon

Orizon is the premier technology innovation hub located in Daura, Katsina State, Nigeria. We are dedicated to bridging the gap between talent and opportunity in Northern Nigeria.

Starting as a software-first hub, we focus on coding, AI, and data science. Our programs are designed to equip young tech enthusiasts with the practical skills they need to succeed in the global tech industry.
